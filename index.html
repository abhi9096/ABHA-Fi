About ABHA Fi

ABHA Fi is an independent, community-built DeFi suite built on Arc Testnet — Circle's stablecoin-native L1. It brings together swaps, liquidity pools, lending and borrowing for USDC, EURC and cirBTC, using USDC-denominated gas for every transaction.

This is a testnet learning project, not an official Circle or Arc product. Feedback, bug reports, and contributions are always welcome!
